## The Bigger Vision (as of May 2026)
This is NOT just a lead gen site. There are multiple layers:

### Layer 1 — Immediate (now through October 2026)
- Get the phone ringing and pass leads to 2-3 trusted crews
- Goal: enough jobs lined up by October that Heather can return and work steadily
- Advertising planned: one specific Brevard neighborhood (TBD — ask Heather)

### Layer 2 — October 2026 Return
- Hands-on training in that neighborhood
- Develop and document full SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)
- Heather already proved the model: wrote everything down, set up the paint, left — it worked
- Painting is repetitive enough to be systematized almost entirely

### Layer 3 — Training System
- Worker chatbot that pulls from SOPs + website content
- Workers can ask it real job questions ("what do I do if the stucco is cracking under the windowsill?")
- Day-before job review system: photo/video of job, go over it, hand off a checklist
- Goal: a new worker can execute correctly with minimal supervision

### Layer 4 — Homeowner Chatbot / Subscription
- Chatbot for homeowners that pulls from the growing website content
- Tiered: free info on the site, possible subscription for DIYers
- The educational content being built now IS the training data for this
- Separate product eventually, or advanced version of this site — TBD

### Layer 5 — Reentry / Second Chance Hiring
- Heather has ties to the prison system in Brevard
- Wants to hire people coming out of incarceration and teach them the trade
- Government programs to use:
  - WOTC (Work Opportunity Tax Credit) — federal tax credit for hiring ex-felons within 1 year of conviction/release
  - Federal Bonding Program — free fidelity bonds for employers hiring ex-offenders
  - Florida may have additional state-level incentives (verify)
- The SOP + training chatbot system is what makes this viable at scale
