# IWCT Business Vision — Full Picture

*Captured May 2026 from Heather's own words. Reference this before any strategic work.*

## The Core Insight
This is not a standard painting company lead gen site. The website content serves
multiple purposes simultaneously: lead generation, homeowner education, future chatbot
training data, worker SOP documentation, and eventually a scalable business platform.

Every piece of content built now has downstream value beyond just getting the phone to ring.

## The Five Layers

### Layer 1 — Lead Generation (Active Now)
- Website gets found → homeowner calls/texts → lead goes to a trusted crew
- Heather manages standards and communication, crews do the work
- Goal: phone ringing before October 2026 return

### Layer 2 — October 2026 Return + SOP Development
- Return to Brevard, work hands-on in a specific neighborhood
- Document everything as SOPs — Heather already proved this works
  ("I wrote everything down, set up the paint, and left — and it worked")
- Painting is repetitive enough to be almost fully systematized
- Day-before job prep: photo/video of job, review with worker, hand off checklist

### Layer 3 — Worker Training System
- SOPs become a training chatbot workers can query on the job
- "What do I do if the stucco is cracking under the windowsill?" → chatbot answers
- Reduces supervision burden, makes onboarding faster and more consistent
- Trained on: SOPs + website content + Heather's documented methods

### Layer 4 — Homeowner Chatbot / DIY Subscription
- The guides and articles being built now become the chatbot's knowledge base
- Free tier: public website content
- Paid/subscription tier: deeper access for DIYers who want guidance
- Potential separate product, or advanced feature of this site
- The people who read 5 guides but never hire = a real addressable audience

### Layer 5 — Second Chance Hiring / Reentry Program
- Heather has existing ties to the prison system in Brevard County
- Wants to hire people coming out of incarceration, teach them the trade
- Makes business sense: motivated workers, loyalty, differentiated workforce pipeline
- Government programs that apply:
  - WOTC (Work Opportunity Tax Credit): federal tax credit for hiring ex-felons
    within 1 year of felony conviction or prison release
  - Federal Bonding Program: free fidelity bonds to protect employers
  - Florida state incentives: verify current programs
- The training system (Layer 3) is what makes this viable — structured onboarding
  means Heather isn't betting on supervision alone

## Why the Layers Fit Together
Content → trust → leads → revenue (Layer 1)
Content → training data → worker chatbot (Layer 3)
Content → homeowner chatbot → subscription revenue (Layer 4)
SOP system → scalable training → reentry program viability (Layer 5)
Each layer reinforces the others. The website is the foundation of all of it.

## The Veteran Neighborhood — Why It's the Perfect Pilot
- 900+ homes in a Brevard County veterans community
- Heather trained service dogs for veterans while she was incarcerated — she has a real personal bond here
- Veterans like deals. Heather will offer better pricing in exchange for being able to train workers there.
- When she returns in October, this neighborhood becomes the live training ground
- Word-of-mouth is how she built her reputation for 5 years — this community will talk
- The inmates + veterans + dogs connection creates a natural, credible pipeline:
  workers trained through the reentry program, deployed in a community that already 
  trusts and supports them — because veterans understand what second chances mean
